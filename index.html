About Me

Hi guys! My name is An Phan, I'm currently a Digital Media student at the University of Houston and I'm 21 years old. I've lived in Houston my entire life, moved to Austin for a year during college and came back home.

I've always wanted to pursue a career with coding and creating websites-- it sounds pretty neat to be able to load up a site and say, "Hey, I made that!" Playing around with Photoshop and Illustrator helped spark that idea and I knew I had to at least try it out.

I have an immense passion and love for dogs (they're just the cutest things to exist!) and spending valuable time with my close family and friends. I hope to travel the world some day to sightsee famous landmarks and other cool things and possibly move out of Texas and the heat!
